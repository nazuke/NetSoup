Map Database Records




Fields:

Title	URL	Pathname	Username	Status




Values:

Title		Title of document, or simple filename


URL			Holds full URL of resource


Pathname	Holds physical pathname of resource on server


Username	Holds name of translator


Status		Describes current status of resource

			Not touched
			In progress
			Translated and not checked
			Translated and checked
			Translated and errors found
			Translated and approved
ound
			Translated and approved
